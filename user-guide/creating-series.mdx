## Overview

Series are the primary organizational structure in MediaStream, allowing you to group related content into seasons and episodes. This guide walks you through creating a new series from scratch.

## Creating Your First Series

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Series List">
    From the Dashboard or sidebar, navigate to the **Series** section to view your existing series library.
    
    You'll see a grid view of all available series with cover images, titles, and descriptions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Create Form">
    Click the **Create New Series** button or navigate to the series creation page.
    
    The breadcrumb trail will show: "Añadir serie" (Add series)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Series Details">
    Fill in the required information:
    
    **Series Name** (Required)
    - Enter a descriptive name for your series
    - This will be displayed to viewers
    - Example: "Tech Tutorials", "Cooking Show Season 1"
    
    **Series Type** (Required)
    - Select from the dropdown menu
    - Currently supports: **Serie** (TV Show)
    - This determines how content is organized and displayed
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit the Form">
    Click the **Añadir serie** (Add series) button to create your series.
    
    - The button shows a spinner animation while processing
    - The form will be disabled during submission
    - Upon success, you'll be redirected to the series detail page
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The form automatically resets after successful submission if you want to create multiple series in a row.
</Tip>

## After Creating a Series

Once your series is created, you'll be taken to the **Series Detail** page where you can:

### View Series Information

- **Cover Image**: Large banner image at the top (aspect ratio: video format)
- **Title**: Your series name prominently displayed
- **Description**: Full series description

### Manage Series Structure

The detail page is divided into two main sections:

<Accordion title="Temporadas (Seasons)">
  The left panel displays all seasons in your series:
  
  - **Season Selector**: Dropdown menu to switch between seasons
  - **Add Season Button**: Plus icon (➕) to create new seasons
  - **Season Cards**: Visual cards for each season with:
    - Season title
    - Season description
    - Cover image
    - Edit button (pencil icon)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Episodios (Episodes)">
  The right panel shows episodes for the selected season:
  
  - Episode thumbnails (25% width)
  - Episode titles and descriptions
  - Clickable to view episode details
</Accordion>

## Adding Seasons to Your Series

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Season Creation">
    From the series detail page, click the **Plus (+)** icon next to the season dropdown.
    
    Breadcrumb path: Series > Temporadas > Añadir temporada
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill Season Information">
    **Season Title** (Required)
    - Example: "Season 1", "2024 Episodes"
    - This helps organize your content chronologically
    
    **Season Description** (Required)
    - Brief overview of what this season contains
    - Example: "Introduction to web development fundamentals"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Season">
    Click **Añadir temporada** (Add season) to save.
    
    The form will reset on success, allowing you to add multiple seasons quickly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Series Management Features

### Image Handling

MediaStream automatically handles cover images:

- Images are served from the CDN: `platform-static.cdn.mdstrm.com`
- Fallback image is provided if no custom image is uploaded
- Hover effects enhance the visual experience

### Navigation

Navigate back to the series list at any time:

- Click **Series** in the breadcrumb trail
- Use the sidebar navigation

### Editing Series

From the series detail page:

- Click the **Edit** (pencil icon) button on any season card
- Update series information as needed

<Note>
Series names and types should be chosen carefully as they affect how content is organized and discovered by viewers.
</Note>
